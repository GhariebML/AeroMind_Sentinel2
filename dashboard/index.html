01 Problem in Egypt

Highways need faster incident detection and mobile AI coverage.

Highway accidents

High-speed corridors can turn small incidents into multi-vehicle emergencies when accidents are detected late.

Delayed incident detection

Manual reporting and limited visibility slow emergency dispatch, especially on long desert highways and ring roads.

Sudden congestion

Stopped vehicles, lane blockage, and accidents can create rapid congestion waves that fixed sensors may miss.

Limited fixed camera coverage

Static infrastructure cannot cover every segment, blind spot, construction zone, or temporary risk area.

Slow emergency response

Without precise, real-time aerial context, emergency services cannot prioritize or route effectively.

Need for AI mobile monitoring

Egypt's smart transportation vision requires intelligence that moves toward risk dynamically.

02 Proposed Solution

Autonomous drone monitoring for smart highway emergency response.

Perception

Real-time incident detection

Detects vehicles, stopped vehicles, pedestrians, road hazards, congestion, blocked lanes, and possible accidents from aerial video.

Persistence

Multi-object tracking

Maintains target identities over time so the system understands movement, stoppage, and incident evolution.

Analytics

Highway risk scoring

The Highway Risk Engine converts detections into 0-100 risk scores, severity labels, and coverage priorities.

Action

Adaptive drone navigation

PPO reinforcement learning moves the drone toward high-risk zones while preserving battery and extending mission duration.

10 Development Roadmap

Next Development Milestones

AeroMind Sentinel currently demonstrates the core product workflow through a functional simulation prototype using structured mock highway events. The next development phase focuses on replacing mock events with live AI model outputs, adding real highway video assets, improving investor-facing pitch visuals, and documenting real deployment screenshots after the Vercel release.

1. Real Highway Video Assets

We will integrate real or licensed highway video footage to demonstrate detection and tracking in more realistic traffic conditions.

2. Live Model Output Integration

The current mock highway events will be replaced with live outputs from YOLOv8, SAHI, BoT-SORT, and the Highway Risk Engine.

3. Pitch Deck Visuals

We will add investor-ready visuals including market size, business model, customer segments, and pilot deployment roadmap.

4. Deployment Screenshots

After the Vercel deployment is finalized, we will add real screenshots of the live product, dashboard, and simulation workflow.

5. TypeScript Build Pipeline

A dedicated TypeScript validation and build pipeline can be added later to improve production reliability and CI/CD readiness.
